有線模式 Wire Mode

1. 插線後自動開始替電池充電。
   Plug the cable and the battery will start charging.
2. 充電時充電指示燈亮起，充飽後熄滅。
   Charging indicator on ESC lights up when charging, off when it's fully charged.
3. 有線模式下支援VIA編輯鍵值。
   Available for configurating on VIA.

-

無線模式 Wireless Mode

持續按壓在Spacebar旁的開關兩秒來開啟無線模式。
Press and hold the switch on right of Spacebar key for 2 seconds to turn on wireless mode.

-

其它指令 Commands

鎖定模式 Lock Mode: LShift + RShift + L
電池電量 Battery Level(available in Win10 after 1903 version): LShift + RShift + V
USB/BT裝置間切換 Toggle Between USB Mode & BLE Mode: LShift + RShift + B
藍牙重置 Module Reset Command: LShift + RShift + R
